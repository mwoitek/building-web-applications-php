Thrash Metal

Thrash Metal is an extreme subgenre of Heavy Metal characterized by its overall aggression and often fast tempo.

Lyrical Subject Matter:

  • Criticism of The Establishment.
  • At times shares a disdain for Christian dogma resembling that of their Black Metal counterparts.
  • Language typically quite direct and denunciatory.

Big Four of Thrash: Anthrax, Megadeth, Metallica, Slayer.
